About Each Dinosaur

T. Rex

adult height: 17 ft.
weight:16,000-19,500 lbs.
time period: 68 mya to 66 mya (million years ago)
desc:

One of the most iconic dinosaurs. Its full name is Tyrannosaurus Rex. It is a carnivore!

Spinosaurus

adult height: 16-23 ft.
weight:7-20 tons
time period: 112-93 mya
desc:

A massive predator with a sail-like back. It hunted fish in rivers and swamps. It could possibly swim.

Brachiosaurus

adult height:40-50 ft
weight:30-60 tons
time period: 154-153 mya
desc:

This dinosaur used its long neck to eat treetop leaves. Its front legs were longer than its hind legs. It had a giraffe-like stance.

Stegosaurus

adult height:9 ft
weight:7 tons
time period:155-150 mya
desc:

Known for plates along its back and spiked tail. It was a slow-moving herbivore. Its brain was very small.

Velocirapter

adult height:2 ft
weight:30–40 lbs
time period:75–71 mya
desc:

Small, feathered, and intelligent hunters. They used sharp claws to catch prey. They were fast and agile.

Triceratops

adult height:9 ft
weight:6–12 tons
time period:68–66 mya
desc:

Known for three horns and a large frill. Its horns were used for defense and dominance fights. It was a plant-eater.
